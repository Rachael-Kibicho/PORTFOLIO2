MY BACKGROUND

At a young age I was interested in numbers and dabbled in problem solving games. At the end of my highschool education, my brother introduced me to programming particularly with tools like python and I found it especially useful with data handling. With the recent emergence of Artificial Intelligence at the time, I was altogether excited about all the possibilities that data analysis skills could unlock and the journey only got more interesting from there.

On entering campus in Kenyatta University, there was an incredible community for student coding and I was all in from the get go. There were many activities that were facilitated by various student clubs for example coding hackathons and peer group teaching in Google Developers Students Club. Activities in Chandaria Business building have also driven me toward innovative ventures and is encouraging me and my peers to create software that is unique and original.
